# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep collaboration professional, respectful, and focused on technical outcomes.

## Expected Behavior

1. Use respectful language in code reviews and discussions.
2. Challenge ideas with evidence, not people.
3. Share context and assumptions clearly.
4. Prioritize user safety and data integrity in decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
2. Deliberate misrepresentation of results or evidence.
3. Repeated disruptive conduct after requested correction.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or block contributions that violate this code. Severe or repeated violations can result in permanent exclusion from collaboration channels.
